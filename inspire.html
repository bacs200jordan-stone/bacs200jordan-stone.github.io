Oprah Winfrey

What makes a hero? A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

Oprah Winfrey is someone that I admire because of the following characteristics.

  • Dedicated Activist - in 1994, Bill Clinton signed a bill into law that Oprah Winfrey could create a database of convicted child abusers.
  • In 2005, Oprah's Angel Network raised more than $50 million for charitable programs.
  • In 2002, Oprah Winfrey was named the first recipient of the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences' Bob Hope Humanitarian Award.
  • Oprah Winfrey became the first Black female billionaire in the United States in 2003.
  • In January 2018, Winfrey became the first African American woman to be honored with the Golden Globes' Cecil B. DeMille Award, for lifetime achievement.

Oprah Winfrey is a hero and an inspiration for so many. She speaks out and helps anyone in need. She also is an extremely successful business woman. She has had multiple shows, books, podcast, magazines, and has won multiple awards for her wealth and charities. She had a tough start to life, but has over came that and became an inspiration for all.
